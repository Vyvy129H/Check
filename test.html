The walk

The walk from Monterosso to Riomaggiore will take you approximately two hours , give or take an houre depending on the weather conditions and your physical shape

What?

cinque Terre comprises five villages: monterosso , Vernazza, Corniglia,Mannarola,and Riomaggiore

Where?

On the northwest cost of the italian riviera,north of the city la Spezia

Price?

The Walk is free
